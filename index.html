Privacy Policy

For MicroPass Chrome Extension

Last updated: [Date]

At MicroPass, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the MicroPass Chrome extension (the "Extension").

1. Information We Collect

The Extension is designed to control your VPN connection and requires access to your login credentials to function. The only personal information collected by this Extension is your login information (e.g., username, password), which is used solely for authenticating your VPN connection. This information is securely stored on our cloud servers.

We do not collect any other personal data beyond what is necessary for the proper operation of the Extension.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your login information is used solely for the purpose of connecting and managing your VPN service. The information is stored securely in our cloud servers, and we do not share it with any third parties unless required by law.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your login information is stored securely on our cloud servers, which are protected by industry-standard security protocols. We use encryption and other measures to safeguard your data both during transmission and while stored on our servers.

4. Data Retention

Your login information is retained on our cloud servers only as long as necessary to provide the service. If you decide to stop using the Extension, we will delete your login information from our servers upon your request.

5. No Tracking of Other Information

We do not track or collect any other personal information related to your use of the Extension, including browsing history, location, or any other identifiable data.

6. Your Rights and Control

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request the deletion of your login information stored on our servers.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time by uninstalling the Extension and contacting us to delete your information from our cloud servers.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at MicroPass Telegram.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
